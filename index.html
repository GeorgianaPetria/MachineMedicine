Smart Cameras For Elderly People

Helping People Stay Independent

Allow your loved ones to stay in their own homes and give them a hand when they most need it. One third of the elder population over the age of 65 falls each year, and the risk of falls increases proportionately with age. By monitoring crucial health metrics we can prevent harm from happening.

What We Do



We are building a smart monitoring system that sits on top of cameras and automatically extracts crucial health metrics. Using a lot of medical knowledge, our system is able to track activities, walking and sleeping patterns, detect falls and alert families when an intervention is needed. You won't need to review hours of video footage and you'll get real time notifications when things are out of place.
