State of the Art Technology

Our smart monitoring system uses state of the art research in computer vision and artificial intelligence in order to automatically extracts crucial health metrics. Using a lot of medical knowledge, the system is able to track activities, walking patterns, detect falls and early disease signs and alert families when an intervention is needed.
There is no human behind the video footage and the decisions are made automatically by the smart algorithms.
